# Sales-Profit-Analysis-Retail for ShopSphere Retail Group
Data analysis project exploring sales vs profit, uncovering loss-making products, and optimizing retail business performance.
## Executive Summary 
  ShopSphere has experienced strong sales growth across regions and product categories. However, profitability has remained inconsistent, with several areas generating losses
  Using Pandas, SQL, and Power BI, this analysis identifies key drivers of revenue and profit, uncovering inefficiencies in discount strategies, product performance, and regional operations. After the analysis, I then recommended strategies to improve profitability.
  
1. Optimize discount strategies 
2. Review loss-making product 
3. Improve regional strategies

## Business Problem 
  Despite increasing sales, ShopSphere Retail Group is facing declining and inconsistent profitability due to poor discount strategies, underperforming products, and regional inefficiencies.

## Methodology
Pandas: Cleaned data, handled missing values. Explored trends, and variable relationships.
SQL: Queried data for detailed insights on product, category, and region performance. Assessed impact of discounts on profit
Power BI: Created interactive dashboards to highlight sales trends, KPIs, and actionable insights for business decision-making
